ABOUT

I dance and choreograph to explore where contemporary dance and pop culture intersect.

Nana Hino is a Japanese contemporary dancer/choreographer who explores the relationship between the humor in contemporary dance and Japanese pop culture by body movement.
Her works appear across various types of media including stage performance, film and virtual reality.
She is also interested in building a physically, mentally and financially sustainable way of producing dance works in order to support artists.
